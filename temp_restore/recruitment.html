Recruitment Process Outsourcing

End to end Recruitment Process Outsourcing for flexible staffing solutions, from speed hiring to strategic workforce design

Enterprise RPO

Complete recruitment ownership with dedicated embedded teams handling sourcing, screening, interviews, offers, onboarding, and strategic reporting via KPI dashboards — ideal for comprehensive outsourcing.

Activity-based RPO

Targeted support for specific recruitment activities like sourcing, screening, and shortlisting, excluding offers; fixed fees per task for hybrid in-house/outsourced models with real-time funnel visibility.

Recruiter on-demand

Flexible, scalable access to specialized recruiters (including onsite options) for surge staffing, agile hiring peaks, or skill gaps, billed monthly per recruiter.
